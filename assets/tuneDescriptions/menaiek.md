**Sign:** put three fingers on your other upper arm (like covering a police badge)

## Background

Introduced by RoR Jerusalem in 2015. “Menaiek” is Hebrew for “cop”.

## Difficulty

* **Low Surdo**: Easy
* **Mid/High Surdo**: Tricky
* **Repi**: Difficult
* **Snare**: Difficult
* **Tamborim**: Difficult
* **Agogô**: Medium
