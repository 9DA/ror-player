**Sign:** draw a triangle in the air with one hand

### Background

Introduced by RoR Kraków in 2016. Based on the traditional German style of music Waltz
([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Waltz_(music))).

### Difficulty

* **Surdos**: Easy
* **Repi**: Easy
* **Snare**: Medium
* **Tamborim**: Easy
* **Agogô**: Easy
